Why Metal3

is the right choice for your bare metal infrastructure needs.

Metal3 provides a unified interface for managing your bare metal hosts, allowing you to easily provision, update, and monitor them from a single location.

Metal3 integrates seamlessly with Kubernetes, allowing you to manage your bare metal hosts using Kubernetes APIs and tools.

With Metal3 you can quickly and easily provision new bare metal hosts, either manually or through automated processes. This allows you to quickly scale your infrastructure as needed.

Metal3 is designed to work with a wide range of hardware platforms, including x86 servers, Arm servers, and more.

Metal3 is an open-source project that is developed and maintained by a community of contributors from around the world. This means that the project is constantly evolving and improving, and that you can benefit from the collective knowledge and expertise of the community.
